Online JSQ Simulator - BlackQueen

BlackQueen (often accompanied with Sheesha) is a popular game amongst the South Asian inhabitants we've come to call "Indians". One can often find such Indians, flocking in groups to JSQ Cafe (or other Sheesha joints) to play BlackQueen.

BlackQueen is a classic team based Indian Playing Card Game based on bidding. This website allows users to create rooms and play the game with their friends over the internet. Please note : Users are responsible to arranging their own Sheesha while playing this online.
